Our Research Vision

Our lab is dedicated to advancing pharmaceutical sciences through an interdisciplinary approach, with a focus on mucosal and respiratory drug delivery. We aim to develop novel therapeutic strategies by understanding drug transport, formulation properties, and biological barriers.

Inside the Lab

Our Major Research Themes

Theme 1: Tailored Drug Release for Enhanced Pharmacological Activity

We develop drug formulations that optimize release profiles to improve therapeutic outcomes, particularly for respiratory and mucosal drug delivery.

Theme 2: Mucosal Barrier Transport Mechanisms

Understanding how drugs interact with and penetrate mucosal barriers to improve bioavailability and efficacy.

Theme 3: Nanoparticle and Biologic Drug Delivery

Exploring novel delivery systems for biologics and nanomedicines targeting mucosal surfaces.

Theme 4: Computational and Predictive Modeling

Developing predictive models to assess drug transport and optimize formulation design.

Theme 5: Impact of Mucosal Composition on Drug Delivery

Investigating how mucosal properties influence drug transport and developing strategies to overcome biological barriers.
